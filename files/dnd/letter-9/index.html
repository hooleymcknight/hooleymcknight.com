Sig's Letter #9

Adrie,

I’m about to talk to the Great Old One. It’s been two years. I don’t know how this is going to go. I’m terrified. Not really of Them…

Adrie, I’m afraid I’ll never get to see you again. They might take me. Take my soul. It...would be kind of like dying. I mean, my body would be gone.

I don’t want to freak you out. I just want you to know the truth. And if this book gets to you and this is the last letter, I don’t want you to try and find me. You won’t be able to. I’ll be gone.

Adrie. You’re the love of my life. You’re the best thing that could have ever happened to me. I wouldn’t trade my life for anyone else’s if you weren’t in it. Everything I’ve ever been through was completely worth it for all of that time with you.

We’ve had so much time. And I’d be lucky to spend all the rest of it with you. But even right now. I just feel happy we’ve had so much time already.

I’m thinking about it. All of it. And I want to just stay here in this letter forever rather than risk what’s next, but I know I can’t. Maybe I could just let myself get a little distracted here with you.

I remember meeting you. How much energy you had. You gave me energy just being around you.

I remember the first time I showed you my true form. You didn’t even flinch. You just hugged me.

I remember that time Naivara left on that trip and we spent every night together. No sneaking or hiding. Just being us and being together.

I remember wrestling around and you cheating by tickling me. Every time. I may have lost every time we wrestled but really, I feel like I won. Just being with you.

I remember arguing about how I should handle my changeling secret. We never agreed but it never got between us. I remember how upset you were with me when I took this deal over my soul. You were right and it was too late but you never loved me any less.

I remember waking up with your arms around me. I remember the feeling of my arms around you.

Fuck, I’m making this harder. I have to go do this. But I need you to know how much I love you. I’ll do whatever I can to get through this and be with you again.

I love you. So. Much. Picture me hugging you really tightly right now. That’s how I’ll be with you, even if I lose my soul and body tonight.

I’m with you, Adrie.

-Sig
