Sig's Letter #3

Adrie,

I’m just thinking about you today. Bored on this boat and there’s nothing to do but think.

Remember when we first kissed? You were visiting, you hadn’t moved in yet. Naivara hit me in the face for saying I liked you. She thought I was going to be a bad influence. Clearly she didn’t know how you were going to grow up. Ha.

Then you told your parents you wanted to have a sleepover in my room. And I freaked, and ran. I was trying to get away but you were pretty fast, following me through the woods. All the way to the big river.

And there it was, the path across. I can’t picture having ever found that if I wasn’t so panicked and desperate. I thought Naivara would kill me if you caught me. I was so afraid of you. In hindsight, we were both kind of crazy for hopping across the rocks in the river like that. We didn’t even know if they would stay put, and the big river is so fast…

But I’m glad we did. Finding that offshoot probably saved my life. Having a spot that was totally safe. Just you and me.

I’m glad I gave up; I’m glad you caught me. That’s one of my favorite memories. The little river was so calming. We were standing on the giant rocks overlooking the little river, totally out of breath. You grabbed the collar of my shirt, one side in each of your hands, and kissed me right on the lips. Then we just stood there, smiling and laughing and catching our breath. That was the first time that normal life didn’t matter anymore.

I wish we could go there now. And just exist. Just us, just be together. And just be.

I miss you.

-Sig
