Hooley McKnight

Sig's Letter #13

Adrie,

This is our second night together. It feels wonderful and right but also so strange still. In a good way, I promise. I just haven’t adjusted yet.

I never pictured this as part of our lives together. Traveling with this group. It was a means to an end for me but now that we’re overlapping the two, them and you… I kind of like it.

I like the way Dearg defends us. I like the way you’re the only person who really likes C’yra somehow. I like the way you and Maeve really get along.

I’m thinking about our day. Just processing it all I guess. You’re totally out, asleep. I missed this. The way you sleep like the world is perfect and peaceful. I wish I could but it’s fine that I do this. I’m just happy being next to you.

Ahhhh you said that to me tonight and I’m still reeling. “By you.” That’s where you’re happy. This is ringing in my ears and it will be when I go to sleep.

I’m glad we talked today. About what happened. You know I’ve not always been the best about facing things head on. But I really want to fix things with us Adrie. I don’t think it’s enough from me to just be here again after I was gone for so long. I need to really be better for you.

I want you to feel safe with me. Safe from me. And secure… I want you to be able to wake up every day and know I’m going to be right there next to you. I can tell this doesn’t feel as easy as it did before but you’re still here anyway, and you say this is where you’re happy.

Adrie, I’m going to work on myself every day. You’re giving me your trust as a gift so I’m going to earn it. Someday you’ll know without a doubt that I’ll never be happy if we’re not together in life. I feel so much more motivated than I normally do. I hope you know I’d do anything for you, babe.

Including not punching C’yra in the face. Unless she starts making comments at you. She’s always getting into our shit and you’ve only been here for a couple of days. Abyss, she probably was eavesdropping when we were talking about that man at the beginning of our watch.

She’s both nosy and secretive. She didn’t tell us about meeting Diesa the bird on her previous watch. Maeve told us but I really don’t think C’yra was going to say anything. Honestly I just don’t get how you like her. I know you see the best in things, and I really love that about you. We just haven’t seen anything good from C’yra yet. But...as always I trust you.

On the plus side, it seems like Dearg took care of that rotting meat smell. I could tell that was bothering you. The walk to Naca was more pleasant with the lavender rotting meat.

I’m still feeling off abou that circus yeti we saw. I don’t know. I wish we had been more...proactive. I think I’m just stuck in this mindset. I wish I was more open and brave.

Well, within reason. If I needed another reason not to like C’yra, I can’t fucking believe she wanted to sacrifice us just to save some coin. She’s so selfish and greedy. I don’t trust her at all. I’m glad the rest of the group agreed to just pay the bandits for Otto. That’s a full ass town I don’t think we could sneak through or defend ourselves from.

Hopefully everything goes smoothly tomorrow getting Otto. I feel good about our group overall. I think we should be okay.

I think I’m going to try and get some sleep now. Here. “By you.” I love you so much Adrie.

-Sig


Well, that was shortlived. Dearg is so funny to me, even when he nearly burns down the whole camp. It was really nice what you did for those twig blights. I love how compassionate you are.
