Hooley McKnight

Sig's Letter #1

Adrie,

Hey Adrie. It’s me, Sig. You probably know from my handwriting. Adrie, I miss you so much. I need you to know I love you. I’ve loved you the whole time I’ve been gone.

Honestly I didn’t plan to leave. I don’t know. I just started running I guess. Then I slipped in the big river. When it finally calmed down enough for me to swim, I was just...so far away. I should’ve tried to come home sooner. I didn’t realize how much time was passing. Then maybe it was too much. Maybe you wouldn’t want to see me again.

Maybe you don’t now. And I get that. I wish I could see you now and even just talk to you about all this, for real and not just in a letter that I can’t even send to you.

I finally went back to the village. Almost two years had passed. You’re gone now. They said you told your parents goodbye almost a year ago. I asked around and figured out you went to this port called Metven. I’m on a boat headed there now. I don’t even know if you’re still there. I hope you are.

Adrie. I’m so sorry I left you alone this whole time. I can’t imagine what I put you through. Did you think I died? Did you think I hated you? Damn, I hope not, but...it’d be awful if I didn’t acknowledge what I put you through.

If you still love me…I’m on my way. I’m trying to find where you went. We can do this, we can find each other and have that life we always talked about, or close enough to it.

We’ll live off the land together. We’ll hunt together again and sit in the woods just watching the world exist around us. I’ll leave notes around for you just like before. You’ll pull my hand and drag me behind a tree at a really inconvenient time. And you’ll tickle me. And I’ll pretend to hate it. We’ll exist just for the sake of existing.

Adrie I hope I’m not too late. I want to rebuild our lives together. Hopefully I’ll see you soon. I love you so much.

-Sig
