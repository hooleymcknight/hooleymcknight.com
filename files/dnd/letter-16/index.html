Sig's Letter #16

Adrie,

I… I can’t believe I hit you. I can’t even begin to cover my remorse. Adrie, I am so sorry. I know we talked about it, but I just feel awful still. I will for a while.

You are...so incredible though. After all that happened, you...think my powers are cool. Great One, are you mind-blowing. I love you so much. Fuck, I’ve missed you. Sitting on your lap today, holding onto you, being held by you… I can’t ever imagine going back to any life without you. I never will. I’ll always stay here, with you.

You’re asleep now, next to me. I just can’t put the image of you hurt out of my head. What a day, and not in a good way. I’m glad it was smooth getting Otto, but damn is he annoying.

And then seeing Maeve completely decimate a wolf was wild. I hated that. I would have talked to her about it, but Dearg was so upset about Buddy going down. I didn’t want Dearg to hurt more than he already did. Thank the Great One, Buddy’s okay now. I really wish we could’ve avoided that fight entirely. I know you do, too.

Valus getting hurt by that trap in the stream was also surprising. And weird. I wonder what that whole goblin and stream situation was about. But, I did feel like I needed to kill him, for the group’s safety.

And...I guess I need to talk to you about some other stuff. We’ll have to find some time to talk soon.

I know we still have the world to catch up on, though, and the rest of our lives together to do it. I love you, babe. I hope you’re sleeping well.

-Sig
