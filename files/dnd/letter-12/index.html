Hooley McKnight

Sig's Letter #12

Adrie,

You’re here. Wow. You’re here with me. I can’t believe this. You still love me, after all this time. You’re incredible.

I woke up somewhat randomly. Maybe I had a dream? I’m not sure. You stirred when I woke up but I didn’t move enough to wake you. So you’re still asleep. Your hand is on my thigh.

Adrie. Abyssal shit, you’re here. I’m so in love with you, Adrie. I wish the night took longer. I don’t want to get up.

I’m sitting here thinking about it...and it’s like you put your love in a metal box and carried it around with you this whole time. Not a single bit of dust.

Things are different. You’re different, I can tell. I’m different. But the way you feel about me is the same. I don’t really understand. But I’m glad.

I just spent the night with you again. In changeling form. I almost thought this would never happen again. I don’t know what’s next but I’m ready for it.

Tonight with you was perfect. I missed you so much. I love you. More than anything else. I’m never leaving again. I wouldn’t make it. I’ll never put you through anything like that again. I’m going to spend every day learning how to be a better partner. All I want overall with my life is to spend it with you. Anything else is just bonus.

I love you, Adrie. I’ll be here when you wake up. Sleep well.

-Sig
