Hooley McKnight

Sig's Letter #8

Adrie,

I just bought a mirror. We’re on our horses, approaching a bridge where we’re going to make camp. We can see it from here but I think we’re still about a mile out. I thought I’d catch you up on my day so far.

After the rock badger fight, Valus and C’yra needed to rest so we tethered their horses to mine and Dearg’s. I volunteered to take Valus so I wouldn’t have to deal with C’yra. I’m going to be honest, I don’t really like her. She’s so negative. It brings out the worst in me. I don’t need any help feeling down. It just doesn’t feel healthy for me to appease her attitude.

Funny enough, Dearg and C’yra’s horses fought a bit and C’yra fell off. I’m only slightly ashamed to say I chuckled a bit.

We untethered the horses and kept going. We passed a group of three travelers cooking at a makeshift camp. One was extremely excited to see us and kept waving. The other two didn’t care. We kept going.

Then we met this super tall orc named Maw. Adrie, this man must have been two and a quarter meters tall! His backpack was even tall; it towered over his head. He was really friendly.

Dearg sold the rock badger pelts I helped him get off the bodies. He barters fairly well. I’ll have to learn a bit about that.

C’yra bought a club. I’m sure I’ll be happy about that later…

I tried to haggle a little myself for this mirror. It went alright, I got the cost down to a silver piece. I’ll need to practice the haggling though. IAnd I’ll need to figure out what this currency is really worth. I don’t have a great idea, as you can imagine. With all my many coins I had back home…

We’re getting closer to the bridge. I might have zoned out a bit looking around at the trees and sky. Heh… I guess just the trees.

This bridge is decently sized. Looks like a ten meter drop down the cliff to the river. The bridge is made of stone. Probably three carriages wide.

We’re pulling our horses off the road now so I have to stop writing. I hope you’re having a fun and relaxing day. I love you, Adrie.

-Sig
