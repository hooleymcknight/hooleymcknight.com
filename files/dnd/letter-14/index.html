Hooley McKnight

Sig's Letter #14

Adrie,

I woke up too early again. I had a dream about that man I killed. There wasn’t much to it… We were just walking through the woods. You, me, Dearg, and Maeve. And we saw that man, just sitting on a rock. We kept walking.

I hate that I took a life, Adrie. I didn’t have a choice really… We were under attack. But I hate it. I wish… I don’t know. I don’t know what I wish.

It’s just a lot to feel.

-Sig
