Hooley McKnight

Sig's Letter #17

Adrie,

Today was calmer than yesterday. That’s strange to say given that today wasn’t calm. What all did we deal with today? We met Khezz the giant spider, survived an entire, full, long fucking day of Otto just rambling on...and then we got ambushed by basically a giant worm, the ankheg.

I hate that Maeve and Otto were talking about baking burns. I hate that it bothered me. I guess I hid from my problems for two years, but didn’t actually sort through any of it.

I guess Otto did have a lot of useful information, about the sea of lava. Although frankly, I find that terrifying. I’m not sure I could be convinced to go, no matter how heat-resistant the boats are. I’m happy enough here, where there are trees, and you.

I keep thinking about Maeve attacking the wolves and now the ankheg. That pink wave of electricity that came out of her today… There’s more to her than meets the eye. I’m curious a little. But I have other things I want to ask her too. I need to stop being a coward and just go talk to her. I’m not good at getting to know people. I sometimes really only feel safe with you.

And I guess Dearg too. He apologized to me today! How wild is that. He had asked if I was going to “do your drawing on that,” talking about the Great One’s sigil. I didn’t know that could have been hurtful. I’m not thinking about things like that, as a defense mechanism. I have a lot to work through.

Unrelated but I’m also still thinking about how Dearg asked Otto about genies. Talkative Otto, the apparent keeper of all knowledge. Seemed like Dearg was curious about his mother. I hope he finds her.

I’m so lucky I found you. Wow. Fuck, I love you. I love having you next to me. It never gets old. I love you, Adrie.

-Sig
